About CBC Talent Hub

CBC Talent Hub is a Kenyan initiative dedicated to empowering children aged 6-14 to discover and nurture their talents in line with the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC).

We provide safe, inclusive spaces where young learners engage in hands-on activities, receive mentorship from industry professionals, and develop skills for real-world impact.
