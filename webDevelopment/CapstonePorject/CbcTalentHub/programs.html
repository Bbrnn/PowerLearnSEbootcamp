Our Visionary Programs

Arts

The Arts program will empower kids to express Kenyan culture through visual arts, crafts, painting, and digital design, with support from schools and communities.

Planned Activities
  • Painting and drawing workshops
  • Craft-making with local materials
  • Digital illustration training
  • Community mural projects
Expected Impact
  • Builds creativity and cultural pride
  • Enhances fine motor skills
  • Prepares for design careers
Partnership Opportunity

Schools can integrate our Arts workshops into their curriculum, and NGOs can sponsor materials.

Partner to Launch This Program

Music

The Music program will foster collaboration and creativity through instruments and digital production, with partnerships from local artists and schools.

Planned Activities
  • Guitar, drums, and traditional instrument training
  • Songwriting and choir groups
  • Digital music production workshops
  • Community performances
Expected Impact
  • Enhances collaboration and expression
  • Develops technical skills
  • Opens music career paths
Partnership Opportunity

Music schools can provide mentors, and investors can fund instruments.

Sports

The Sports program will build discipline and teamwork through football, basketball, and athletics, with support from schools and sports organizations.

Planned Activities
  • Football and basketball training
  • Track and field events
  • Swimming lessons
  • Inter-school tournaments
Expected Impact
  • Improves physical health
  • Develops leadership skills
  • Encourages sports passion
Partnership Opportunity

Sports clubs can provide coaches, and government can fund facilities.

Technology

The Technology program will prepare kids for tech careers through coding and robotics, with partnerships from tech companies and schools.

Planned Activities
  • Coding websites and games
  • Robotics workshops
  • Digital literacy training
  • Hackathons and showcases
Expected Impact
  • Builds problem-solving skills
  • Prepares for tech careers
  • Fosters innovation
Partnership Opportunity

Tech firms can sponsor equipment, and schools can host workshops.
