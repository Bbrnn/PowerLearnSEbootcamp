About CBC Talent Hub

CBC Talent Hub is an innovative, youth-driven initiative based in Kenya, designed to help children discover, nurture, and develop their talents through a structured learning environment aligned with Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC).

We believe talent development goes beyond academics, creating a safe, inclusive, and inspiring space where children can thrive through hands-on learning, creative expression, and collaboration.

Our Vision

To empower the next generation of African children to be confident, skilled, and purpose-driven individuals through talent development and experiential learning.

Our Mission

  • Discover natural talents across multiple disciplines.
  • Learn through hands-on, project-based activities.
  • Develop practical skills for real-world application.
  • Receive mentorship from industry professionals.

What We Offer

Arts

Explore visual arts, crafts, painting, and digital design, allowing children to express their stories and Kenyan culture through creative mediums, from traditional art to digital illustration.

Music

Learn rhythm, melody, instruments, and digital music production in a collaborative environment, fostering teamwork and creativity.

Sports

Develop discipline, teamwork, and leadership through team and solo sports like football, basketball, athletics, and swimming, emphasizing physical fitness and fun.

Technology

Introduce kids to coding, robotics, and digital literacy, preparing them for tech careers by building websites, designing games, and more.

Who We Serve

  • Children aged 6 to 14, aligned with Kenya's CBC.
  • Schools looking to enrich co-curricular offerings.
  • Parents seeking talent development opportunities.
  • Communities invested in youth leadership and innovation.

Our Approach

We combine physical talent hubs with a hybrid model, offering both in-person and online access to resources, mentorship, and tools. Our approach includes:

  • Weekly talent development sessions and bootcamps.
  • Holiday programs for school breaks.
  • Digital learning content for remote participation.
  • Community showcases and exhibitions to present talents.

Why CBC Talent Hub?

In a world requiring creativity, adaptability, and practical skills, CBC Talent Hub equips children to excel in academics, arts, sports, and technology, empowering them to:

  • Break barriers and build self-confidence.
  • Develop leadership and entrepreneurial skills.
  • Harness passions to create positive change.
